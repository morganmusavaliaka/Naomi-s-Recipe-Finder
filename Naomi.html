Instructions

Part 1: Make the Carrot Cake Preheat Oven and Prepare Pans: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9x13 inch pan or two 8 or 9-inch round pans. For round pans, lining the bottoms with parchment paper also helps prevent sticking. Mix Dry Ingredients: In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt until well combined. Mix Wet Ingredients: In a separate large bowl, beat the sugar, oil, eggs, and vanilla extract together with an electric mixer on low speed until smooth and well blended. Combine Wet and Dry: Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture and beat on low speed for about a minute, or just until combined. Be careful not to overmix the batter. Add Carrots and Nuts: Stir in the grated carrots and chopped nuts (if using) with a spatula until evenly distributed. Bake: Pour the batter into the prepared pan(s). For a 9x13 inch pan, bake for 40 to 45 minutes. For round pans, bake for 30 to 35 minutes. The cake is done when a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean or with a few moist crumbs. Cool Completely: Allow the cake to cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then turn it out onto a wire rack to cool completely. The cake must be entirely cool before frosting.
